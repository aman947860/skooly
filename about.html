How We Started

SkoolyAI Expert didn't start in a boardroom or with a pitch deck. It started with a simple observation: AI companies don't have a data problem—they have a delivery problem.

While everyone was building platforms and fancy UX, we went on-ground. Four cities. Over 1,000 face-to-face meetings with PhDs, engineers, finance professionals, and domain experts. We didn't collect emails—we built relationships. We didn't promise work—we delivered it.

Within 6 months, we had 500+ active contributors, 5 completed projects, and $30K in revenue—all without a formal platform. How? Because we treated AI data creation as a supply chain problem, not a software problem.

Our advantage is simple: we have the machine loaded before projects even start. While competitors scramble to find workers after landing clients, our giant supply volume is already trained, verified, and ready to execute.

That's why we deliver 2× faster and 45% cheaper than traditional vendors. It's not magic—it's operations.
