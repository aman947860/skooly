Humari Shuruat Kaise Hui

SkoolyAI Expert kisi boring boardroom meeting ya fancy pitch deck se shuru nahi hua tha. Yeh ek simple observation se shuru hua: AI companies ke paas data ki kami nahi hai—balki use sahi time pe deliver karne ki dikkat hai.

Jab sab log fancy platforms aur UI/UX banane me busy the, hum seedhe ground pe utar gaye. Char bade cities. 1,000 se zyada face-to-face meetings—PhDs, software engineers, finance professionals aur domain experts ke saath. Humne sirf email collect nahi kiye—sache relationships banaye. Khali wade nahi kiye—kaam deliver kiya.

Sirf 6 mahine ke andar, humare paas 500+ active contributors, 5 completed projects aur $30K ki revenue thi—wo bhi bina kisi formal platform ke. Kaise? Kyunki humne AI data creation ko ek software problem nahi, balki supply chain problem ki tarah solve kiya.

Humara fayda simple hai: project shuru hone se pehle hi humara system ready rehta hai. Jab doosre log client milne ke baad contributors dhoondhte hain, tab humari badi community pehle se hi trained, verified aur execute karne ke liye taiyar rehti hai.

Isi wajah se hum baaki vendors se 2× fast aur 45% sasta kaam deliver karte hain. Yeh koi jaadu nahi hai—pure operations ka kamaal hai.
